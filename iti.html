ITI Courses

Industrial Training Institute (ITI) courses provide practical and skill-based technical education. These programs focus on hands-on training and prepare students for employment in industries, workshops, manufacturing units, and service sectors.

Popular ITI Trades

  • Electrician
  • Fitter
  • Welder
  • Mechanic

Salary Scope

Freshers can earn approximately ₹2 LPA – ₹5 LPA depending on the trade and industry. Experienced professionals can earn ₹6 LPA – ₹12 LPA or more with specialized skills.

Skills Required

  • Technical Skills
  • Machine Handling
  • Practical Knowledge
  • Troubleshooting Ability
  • Safety Awareness
  • Teamwork
  • Problem Solving

Future Scope

ITI graduates are highly demanded in manufacturing, construction, automobile, electrical, railway, oil & gas and government sectors. With experience and certifications, they can advance to technician, supervisor, contractor or entrepreneur roles.
