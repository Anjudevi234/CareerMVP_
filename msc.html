M.Sc

Master of Science (M.Sc) is a postgraduate degree focused on advanced scientific knowledge, research, innovation and specialization in science-related fields.

Brief Description

M.Sc is a two-year postgraduate degree designed for students who wish to gain advanced expertise in scientific disciplines such as Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, Computer Science, Statistics and Environmental Science.

Fresher Salary

Fresh M.Sc graduates can earn ₹4 LPA – ₹8 LPA depending on specialization, research experience and employer.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals can earn ₹8 LPA – ₹30 LPA or more in Research, Data Science, Biotechnology and Scientific Industries.

Future Scope

M.Sc graduates have strong opportunities in Research & Development, Biotechnology, Data Science, Academia, Environmental Science and Scientific Organizations. The degree also serves as a pathway to PhD and advanced research careers.
