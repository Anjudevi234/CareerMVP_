Commerce

Study business, trade, finance and accounting to build careers in banking, management, entrepreneurship and corporate sectors.

Brief Description

Commerce is the field of study that focuses on business, trade, finance, accounting, economics and management. It prepares students for careers in banking, business, corporate management, entrepreneurship and financial services.

Fresher Salary

Fresh graduates can earn approximately ₹3 LPA – ₹7 LPA depending on qualifications, skills and the organization.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals such as Chartered Accountants, Financial Managers and Investment Bankers can earn ₹8 LPA – ₹30 LPA or more.

Future Scope

Commerce offers excellent career opportunities in banking, finance, accounting, taxation, auditing, investment management and entrepreneurship. With the growth of global businesses and financial markets, demand for commerce professionals continues to increase.
