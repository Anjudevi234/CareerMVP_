B.Sc

Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) is a professional undergraduate degree focused on scientific knowledge, research, technology and analytical skills.

Brief Description

B.Sc is a three-year undergraduate degree designed for students interested in science, research and technology. Students can specialize in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, Computer Science and many other scientific fields.

Fresher Salary

Fresh B.Sc graduates can earn ₹2.5 LPA – ₹6 LPA depending on specialization, skills and employer.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals can earn ₹6 LPA – ₹25 LPA or more in Research, Data Science, Biotechnology, IT and Scientific Industries.

B.Sc graduates have excellent opportunities in Research, Biotechnology, Healthcare, Data Science, Education, Environmental Science and Information Technology. Higher studies like M.Sc and PhD can significantly improve career growth and specialization.
