Junior Engineer

Assist in designing, testing, implementing, and maintaining engineering projects and systems.

Brief Description

A Junior Engineer supports senior engineers in planning, designing, developing, testing, and maintaining engineering projects. They work in industries such as construction, manufacturing, power generation, telecommunications, and IT.

Fresher Salary

Freshers can earn approximately ₹3 LPA – ₹6 LPA depending on their engineering discipline, skills, and employer.

Experienced Salary

Experienced Engineers can earn ₹8 LPA – ₹20 LPA or more. Senior Engineers, Project Managers, and Engineering Heads often receive significantly higher compensation.

Future Scope

Junior Engineers have excellent growth opportunities across construction, manufacturing, power, automobile, aerospace, and technology sectors. With experience and higher education, they can advance to Senior Engineer, Project Manager, Engineering Manager, and leadership roles.
