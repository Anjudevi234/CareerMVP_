MBBS

Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) is the primary medical degree required to become a doctor and serve in healthcare.

Brief Description

MBBS is a professional undergraduate medical degree that trains students in medicine, surgery, diagnosis, treatment and patient care. It is one of the most respected and rewarding professions worldwide.

Fresher Salary

Fresh MBBS graduates can earn ₹6 LPA – ₹12 LPA depending on hospital, location and specialization.

Experienced Salary

Experienced doctors and specialists can earn ₹15 LPA – ₹1 Crore+ annually depending on their specialization, experience and practice.

Future Scope

Healthcare is one of the fastest-growing sectors globally. MBBS graduates have excellent opportunities in hospitals, private practice, research, telemedicine, public health and medical education.
