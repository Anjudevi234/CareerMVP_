Mechanic

Diagnose, repair, maintain, and service vehicles, machinery, and mechanical systems.

Brief Description

A Mechanic is a skilled professional who inspects, diagnoses, repairs, and maintains automobiles, industrial machinery, engines, and mechanical equipment. Mechanics play a vital role in ensuring machines and vehicles operate safely and efficiently.

Fresher Salary

Freshers can earn approximately ₹2 LPA – ₹4.5 LPA depending on qualifications, skills, and the industry they join.

Experienced Salary

Experienced Mechanics can earn ₹5 LPA – ₹12 LPA or more. Professionals specializing in heavy machinery, industrial maintenance, or international projects may earn significantly higher salaries.

Future Scope

Mechanics are in demand across the automobile, manufacturing, transportation, construction, and industrial sectors. With experience and additional certifications, Mechanics can advance to supervisory, engineering support, workshop management, and specialized maintenance roles.
