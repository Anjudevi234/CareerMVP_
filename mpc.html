MPC (Mathematics, Physics & Chemistry)

MPC stands for Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry. It is one of the most popular streams after 10th and is ideal for students interested in Engineering, Technology, Computer Science, Research and Defense careers.

Subjects

  • Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry

Salary Scope

Fresh graduates can earn approximately ₹3 LPA – ₹12 LPA depending on their skills, specialization and organization. Experienced professionals can earn ₹15 LPA – ₹40 LPA or more.

Skills Required

  • Logical Thinking
  • Problem Solving
  • Analytical Skills
  • Mathematical Ability
  • Communication Skills
  • Technical Skills

Future Scope

MPC students have excellent opportunities in Engineering, Information Technology, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cyber Security, Robotics, Space Research and Government sectors.
