Medical Lab Technician

Perform laboratory tests and assist doctors in diagnosing diseases accurately.

Brief Description

Medical Laboratory Technicians (MLTs) are healthcare professionals who perform laboratory tests on blood, urine, tissue and other body samples. Their work helps doctors diagnose diseases, monitor treatments and maintain patient health.

MLTs play a critical role in hospitals, diagnostic centers, research laboratories and healthcare institutions by ensuring accurate and reliable laboratory results.

Future Scope

The healthcare and diagnostic sectors are expanding rapidly, increasing the demand for skilled Medical Lab Technicians. Advances in medical technology and diagnostic testing are creating new opportunities in laboratories and research facilities.

MLTs can also pursue specialized careers in pathology, microbiology, molecular diagnostics and laboratory management.
