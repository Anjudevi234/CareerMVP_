Mechanical Engineering

Design, develop, manufacture and maintain machines, engines and mechanical systems used in industries worldwide.

Brief Description

Mechanical Engineering is one of the oldest and most versatile engineering disciplines. It focuses on the design, production, operation and maintenance of machines, engines, vehicles, industrial equipment and manufacturing systems.

Fresher Salary

Fresh Mechanical Engineers can earn approximately ₹3 LPA – ₹7 LPA depending on skills, company and location.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals can earn ₹8 LPA – ₹25 LPA or more. Senior engineers, project managers and specialists often earn higher salaries.

Mechanical Engineers are in demand across manufacturing, automobile, aerospace, energy, robotics and construction industries. Emerging technologies such as automation, electric vehicles and robotics are creating new opportunities worldwide.
