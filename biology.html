Biology

Study living organisms, life processes, genetics and ecosystems.

Brief Description

Biology is the branch of science that studies living organisms, their structure, functions, growth, evolution and interactions with the environment. It forms the foundation for careers in medicine, biotechnology, genetics, healthcare and environmental science.
