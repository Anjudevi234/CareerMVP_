MEC (Mathematics, Economics & Commerce)

MEC stands for Mathematics, Economics and Commerce. It is an excellent stream for students interested in Business, Finance, Banking, Accounting, Economics, Management and Entrepreneurship careers.

  • Mathematics
  • Economics
  • Commerce

Salary Scope

Fresh graduates can earn approximately ₹3 LPA – ₹8 LPA. Experienced professionals, Chartered Accountants, Business Analysts and Bank Officers can earn ₹10 LPA – ₹30 LPA or more.

Skills Required

  • Leadership Skills
  • Communication Skills
  • Analytical Thinking
  • Financial Knowledge
  • Problem Solving
  • Decision Making
  • Management Skills

Future Scope

MEC students have strong career opportunities in Banking, Finance, Accounting, Taxation, Investment Management, Business Analytics, Entrepreneurship and Corporate Management. The demand for finance and business professionals continues to grow globally.
