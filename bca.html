BCA

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a professional undergraduate degree focused on computer applications, software development and information technology.

Brief Description

BCA is a three-year undergraduate degree designed for students interested in software development, programming, databases, web technologies and IT services. It is one of the most popular courses for building a career in the technology industry.

Fresher Salary

Fresh BCA graduates can earn ₹3 LPA – ₹7 LPA depending on skills, projects, certifications and employer.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals can earn ₹8 LPA – ₹30 LPA or more in Software Development, Cloud Computing, Data Science and Cyber Security.
