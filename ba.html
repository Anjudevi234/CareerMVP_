B.A

Bachelor of Arts (B.A) is a popular undergraduate degree focused on Humanities, Social Sciences, Languages, Communication and Creative Studies.

Brief Description

B.A is a three-year undergraduate degree designed for students interested in History, Economics, Political Science, Psychology, Sociology, English Literature and other humanities subjects. It helps develop analytical, communication and critical thinking skills.

Fresher Salary

Fresh B.A graduates can earn ₹2.5 LPA – ₹5 LPA depending on skills, specialization and employer.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals can earn ₹6 LPA – ₹20 LPA or more in Media, Education, Human Resources, Government Services and Corporate Communication.
