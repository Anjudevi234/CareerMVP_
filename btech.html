B.Tech Engineering

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) is a professional undergraduate engineering degree that provides technical knowledge and practical skills in various engineering disciplines.

Brief Description

B.Tech is a four-year undergraduate engineering program designed to prepare students for careers in technology, innovation, research and industrial development. Students can specialize in various engineering branches based on their interests.

Fresher Salary

Fresh B.Tech graduates can earn between ₹3 LPA – ₹8 LPA depending on specialization, skills, certifications and employer.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals can earn ₹10 LPA – ₹50 LPA or more in fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cloud Computing and Product Development.

B.Tech graduates have excellent future opportunities in Software Development, Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, Machine Learning, Cyber Security, Cloud Computing, Renewable Energy and Industrial Automation. Engineering remains one of the most sought-after professions globally.
