Accountant

Manage financial records, prepare reports, handle taxation, and support business decision-making.

Brief Description

An Accountant is responsible for recording, analyzing, and maintaining financial transactions of individuals, businesses, and organizations. They prepare financial statements, manage budgets, ensure tax compliance, and provide valuable financial insights to support decision-making.

Fresher Salary

₹2.5 LPA – ₹5 LPA depending on qualifications, skills, location, and organization.

Experienced Salary

Professionals with 5–10 years of experience can earn ₹6 LPA – ₹15 LPA or more. Chartered Accountants and Finance Managers may earn significantly higher salaries.

Future Scope

Accountants remain in high demand across industries due to growing business operations, financial regulations, and tax requirements. Professionals with expertise in auditing, taxation, financial analysis, and international accounting standards can find excellent opportunities both in India and abroad.
