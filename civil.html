Civil Engineering

Design, construct and maintain buildings, roads, bridges, dams and other infrastructure projects.

Brief Description

Civil Engineering is a branch of engineering that focuses on planning, designing, constructing and maintaining infrastructure such as buildings, highways, bridges, tunnels, railways, airports and water supply systems.

Fresher Salary

Fresh Civil Engineers can earn approximately ₹3 LPA – ₹6 LPA depending on company, location and project type.

Experienced Salary

Experienced Civil Engineers can earn ₹8 LPA – ₹20 LPA or more. Senior project managers, consultants and structural specialists often earn higher salaries.

Future Scope

Civil Engineering offers excellent opportunities in construction, infrastructure development, smart cities, transportation systems and environmental projects. The demand for skilled civil engineers continues to grow with urbanization and large-scale infrastructure investments.
