Welder

Join, fabricate and repair metal structures using various welding techniques.

Brief Description

A Welder is a skilled professional who joins, cuts and repairs metal parts using welding equipment and techniques. Welders work in construction, manufacturing, shipbuilding, automobile, aerospace and heavy engineering industries.

Fresher Salary

Freshers can earn approximately ₹2 LPA – ₹4 LPA depending on their skills, certifications and employer.

Experienced Salary

Experienced Welders can earn ₹5 LPA – ₹12 LPA or more. Professionals with international certifications and specialized welding expertise may earn significantly higher salaries.

Future Scope

Welders are in demand across construction, manufacturing, oil & gas, shipbuilding, automobile and infrastructure sectors. With experience and certifications, welders can advance to supervisory, inspection, quality control and international project roles.
