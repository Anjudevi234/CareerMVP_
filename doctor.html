Doctor

Diagnose, treat and prevent diseases while improving the health and well-being of patients.

Brief Description

A Doctor is a highly trained healthcare professional responsible for diagnosing illnesses, providing treatments, prescribing medications and promoting healthy lifestyles. Doctors play a vital role in society by helping people maintain physical and mental well-being.

Medicine is one of the most respected and rewarding professions. Doctors work in hospitals, clinics, research centers, medical colleges and public health organizations.

₹6 LPA – ₹12 LPA

Newly qualified doctors generally start with internships, junior resident positions or hospital appointments.

Experienced Salary

₹15 LPA – ₹50+ LPA

Specialists and senior doctors in reputed hospitals can earn significantly higher salaries depending on their expertise and experience.

Future Scope

Healthcare is one of the fastest-growing sectors globally. The demand for qualified doctors continues to increase due to population growth, advancements in medical technology and greater healthcare awareness.

Specializations such as Cardiology, Neurology, Oncology, Radiology and Robotic Surgery offer excellent career prospects and earning potential.
