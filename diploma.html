Diploma Courses

Diploma courses provide technical education, practical skills, and industry-focused training. They are ideal for students who wish to enter engineering and technical fields after completing their 10th standard.

Salary Scope

Diploma graduates can earn approximately ₹2.5 LPA – ₹6 LPA as freshers. Experienced professionals and supervisors can earn ₹8 LPA – ₹15 LPA or more depending on specialization and industry.

Skills Required

  • Technical Knowledge
  • Problem Solving
  • Teamwork
  • Communication Skills
  • Machine Handling
  • Analytical Thinking
  • Safety Awareness

Future Scope

Diploma holders have excellent opportunities in manufacturing, construction, IT, automobile, electrical and industrial sectors. They can pursue higher education through lateral entry programs or secure technical positions in both private and government organizations.
