Chemistry

Study substances, reactions and chemical processes that shape the world around us.

Brief Description

Chemistry is the branch of science that studies matter, its composition, properties, structure and chemical reactions. It plays a vital role in medicine, pharmaceuticals, engineering, environmental science and industrial production.
