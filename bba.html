BBA

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a professional undergraduate degree focused on business management, leadership, marketing and entrepreneurship.

Brief Description

BBA is a three-year undergraduate degree designed to provide students with strong knowledge in business management, marketing, finance, human resources and entrepreneurship. It is one of the best courses for students interested in management and corporate careers.

Fresher Salary

Fresh BBA graduates can earn ₹3 LPA – ₹7 LPA depending on skills, internships and employer.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals can earn ₹8 LPA – ₹30 LPA or more in Management, Marketing, Human Resources and Business Consulting.

Future Scope

BBA graduates have excellent career opportunities in Business Management, Marketing, Human Resources, Finance, Entrepreneurship and Corporate Leadership. Pursuing an MBA after BBA significantly enhances career growth and salary potential.
