MBA

Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a postgraduate degree focused on management, leadership, business strategy and entrepreneurship.

Brief Description

MBA is a two-year postgraduate degree that develops leadership, management and decision-making skills. It is one of the most sought-after qualifications for careers in business, finance, marketing and entrepreneurship.

Fresher Salary

Fresh MBA graduates can earn ₹5 LPA – ₹12 LPA depending on specialization, college reputation and employer.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals can earn ₹12 LPA – ₹50 LPA or more in Management, Consulting, Finance, Marketing and Leadership roles.

MBA graduates have excellent opportunities in corporate leadership, consulting, entrepreneurship, finance, marketing and international business. MBA remains one of the most valuable qualifications for career growth and executive-level positions.
