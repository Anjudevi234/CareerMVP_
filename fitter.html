Fitter

Assemble, install, maintain and repair mechanical equipment, machines and industrial systems.

Brief Description

A Fitter is a skilled technician who assembles, installs, repairs and maintains machinery, equipment and mechanical systems used in industries, manufacturing units and workshops. Fitters play an important role in ensuring smooth industrial operations.

Fresher Salary

Freshers can earn approximately ₹2 LPA – ₹4 LPA depending on their skills, certifications and industry.

Experienced Salary

Experienced Fitters can earn ₹4 LPA – ₹10 LPA or more. Specialized professionals working in large industries and overseas projects can earn significantly higher salaries.

Future Scope

Fitters are highly demanded in manufacturing, automobile, construction, power generation, oil & gas and heavy engineering industries. With experience, fitters can move into supervisory, maintenance and technical management roles.
