Technician

Install, maintain, troubleshoot, and repair equipment, machinery, and technical systems.

Brief Description

A Technician is a skilled professional who installs, operates, maintains, and repairs technical equipment and systems. Technicians work in industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, electronics, telecommunications, information technology, and construction.

Fresher Salary

Freshers typically earn between ₹2 LPA – ₹4.5 LPA, depending on industry, skills, and qualifications.

Experienced Salary

Experienced Technicians can earn ₹5 LPA – ₹12 LPA or more. Those with specialized certifications and supervisory roles may receive higher salaries.

Future Scope

Technicians are in high demand across industries due to increasing automation, technological advancements, and industrial growth. With experience and additional qualifications, technicians can advance to supervisory, engineering, and management positions.
