Business Careers

Business careers focus on management, finance, marketing, entrepreneurship and organizational leadership across various industries.

Brief Description

Business professionals help organizations achieve their goals through strategic planning, financial management, marketing, operations and leadership. Business careers offer excellent growth opportunities in both private and public sectors.

Fresher Salary

Fresh business graduates can earn ₹3 LPA – ₹8 LPA depending on qualifications, skills and employer.

Experienced Salary

Experienced business professionals can earn ₹10 LPA – ₹50 LPA or more in management, consulting, finance and leadership positions.

Future Scope

Business careers continue to grow due to globalization, digital transformation and increasing demand for skilled managers and business leaders. Opportunities exist in corporates, startups, government organizations and international companies.
