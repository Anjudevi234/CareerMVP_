M.Com

Master of Commerce (M.Com) is a postgraduate degree focused on Accounting, Finance, Taxation, Banking, Business Management and Commerce-related studies.

Brief Description

M.Com is a two-year postgraduate degree designed for students who wish to gain advanced knowledge in Commerce, Accounting, Finance, Taxation, Economics and Business Management. It is an excellent option for students aiming for careers in banking, finance and corporate sectors.

Fresher Salary

Fresh M.Com graduates can earn ₹3.5 LPA – ₹7 LPA depending on specialization, skills and employer.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals can earn ₹8 LPA – ₹25 LPA or more in Banking, Finance, Accounting, Auditing and Taxation sectors.

Future Scope

M.Com graduates have strong opportunities in Banking, Financial Services, Taxation, Auditing, Investment Management and Corporate Finance. Pursuing professional qualifications such as CA, CMA or MBA can significantly enhance career growth.
