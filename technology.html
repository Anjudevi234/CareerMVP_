Technology Careers

Technology is one of the fastest-growing industries, offering exciting opportunities in software development, artificial intelligence, cyber security, cloud computing and data science.

Brief Description

Technology careers involve designing, developing and maintaining software systems, digital platforms, computer networks and innovative technologies that power modern businesses and industries.

Fresher Salary

Fresh graduates entering technology careers can earn between ₹3 LPA and ₹8 LPA depending on skills, projects, certifications and company.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals can earn ₹10 LPA to ₹50 LPA or more in fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing and Cyber Security.

Future Scope

Technology continues to expand rapidly due to Artificial Intelligence, Automation, Cloud Computing, Internet of Things (IoT) and Digital Transformation. Demand for technology professionals is expected to remain strong for decades.
