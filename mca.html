MCA

Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is a postgraduate degree focused on advanced software development, computer applications and emerging technologies.

Brief Description

MCA is a two-year postgraduate degree designed for students who want advanced knowledge in software engineering, programming, databases, cloud computing and artificial intelligence. It prepares graduates for high-level careers in the IT industry.

Fresher Salary

Fresh MCA graduates can earn ₹4 LPA – ₹10 LPA depending on skills, projects, certifications and employer.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals can earn ₹10 LPA – ₹40 LPA or more in Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Software Development and Data Science.
