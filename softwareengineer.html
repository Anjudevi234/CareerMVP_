Software Engineer

Design, develop, test and maintain software applications and systems.

Brief Description

Software Engineering is one of the most popular technology careers. Software Engineers create websites, mobile applications, enterprise systems, cloud platforms, AI solutions and software products used by millions of people worldwide.

Fresher Salary

₹3 LPA – ₹8 LPA depending on skills, company and location. Top product companies may offer ₹10 LPA or more.

Experienced Salary

Professionals with 5–10 years of experience can earn ₹10 LPA – ₹35 LPA. Senior engineers, architects and managers may earn significantly higher salaries.

Future Scope

The demand for Software Engineers continues to grow due to Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Data Science, Cyber Security, IoT and Digital Transformation. It remains one of the highest-paying and fastest-growing careers globally.
