Mathematics

Study numbers, logic, calculations and problem-solving for science, technology and business careers.

Brief Description

Mathematics is a fundamental subject that deals with numbers, patterns, formulas, calculations and logical reasoning. It forms the foundation for engineering, computer science, data science, finance, economics and research careers.
