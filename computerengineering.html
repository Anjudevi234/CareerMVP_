Computer Engineering

Design, develop and maintain computer systems, software applications and modern digital technologies.

Brief Description

Computer Engineering combines computer science and electronics to design computer hardware, software systems, networks and digital technologies. It is one of the fastest-growing fields with opportunities in IT, AI, Cyber Security and Cloud Computing.

Fresher Salary

Fresh Computer Engineering graduates can earn ₹3 LPA – ₹8 LPA depending on skills, certifications and company.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals can earn ₹10 LPA – ₹40 LPA or more in fields like Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Cyber Security and Software Development.

Future Scope

Computer Engineering has excellent future scope due to Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, Cloud Computing, Cyber Security, IoT and Digital Transformation. It remains one of the highest-demand career fields globally.
