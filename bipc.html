BiPC (Biology, Physics & Chemistry)

BiPC stands for Biology, Physics and Chemistry. It is one of the most popular streams for students interested in Medicine, Healthcare, Pharmacy, Nursing, Biotechnology and Life Sciences careers.

Subjects

  • Biology
  • Physics
  • Chemistry

Popular Career Opportunities

Diagnose diseases, provide treatment, perform surgeries and improve patient health.

Salary Scope

Fresh graduates can earn approximately ₹3 LPA – ₹8 LPA depending on the course and profession. Doctors, specialists and healthcare professionals with experience can earn ₹10 LPA – ₹50 LPA or more.

Skills Required

  • Patience
  • Communication Skills
  • Medical Knowledge
  • Critical Thinking
  • Problem Solving
  • Empathy
  • Observation Skills

Future Scope

BiPC students have excellent opportunities in Medicine, Healthcare, Pharmacy, Nursing, Biotechnology, Genetics, Clinical Research, Public Health and Medical Technology sectors. The healthcare industry continues to grow rapidly, creating strong demand for qualified professionals.
