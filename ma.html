M.A

Master of Arts (M.A) is a postgraduate degree focused on Humanities, Social Sciences, Languages, Literature, History, Political Science and related disciplines.

Brief Description

M.A is a two-year postgraduate degree designed for students who wish to gain advanced knowledge in Humanities and Social Sciences. It develops analytical thinking, research skills, communication abilities and subject expertise.

Fresher Salary

Fresh M.A graduates can earn ₹3 LPA – ₹6 LPA depending on specialization, skills and employer.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals can earn ₹6 LPA – ₹20 LPA or more in Education, Media, Government Services, Research and Administration.
