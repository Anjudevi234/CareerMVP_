B.Com

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) is a popular undergraduate degree focused on accounting, finance, taxation, banking and business management.

Brief Description

B.Com is a three-year undergraduate degree designed to provide students with knowledge of commerce, finance, accounting, economics and business operations. It is ideal for students interested in banking, taxation and corporate careers.

Fresher Salary

Fresh B.Com graduates can earn ₹2.5 LPA – ₹6 LPA depending on skills, certifications and employer.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals can earn ₹8 LPA – ₹25 LPA or more in Accounting, Banking, Finance, Investment and Taxation sectors.

Future Scope

B.Com graduates have strong career prospects in Banking, Finance, Taxation, Auditing, Corporate Management and Entrepreneurship. Professional qualifications like CA, CMA and MBA further enhance career opportunities.
