Business Analyst

Analyze business processes, identify opportunities, and help organizations improve efficiency and profitability.

Brief Description

A Business Analyst acts as a bridge between business stakeholders and technical teams. They analyze business needs, identify problems, gather requirements, and recommend solutions that improve business performance and productivity.

Fresher Salary

Freshers can earn approximately ₹4 LPA – ₹8 LPA depending on their skills, qualifications, and the organization they join.

Experienced Salary

Experienced Business Analysts can earn ₹10 LPA – ₹25 LPA or more. Senior Analysts, Consultants, and Managers often receive higher compensation.

Future Scope

Business Analysts are in high demand across industries including banking, IT, healthcare, e-commerce, consulting, and finance. As organizations rely more on data-driven decisions, the demand for skilled Business Analysts continues to grow globally.
