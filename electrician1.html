Electrician

Install, maintain, troubleshoot, and repair electrical systems and equipment.

Brief Description

An Electrician is a skilled professional who installs, repairs, tests, and maintains electrical wiring, equipment, and systems. Electricians work in residential, commercial, industrial, and construction environments to ensure safe and efficient electrical operations.

Fresher Salary

Freshers can earn approximately ₹2 LPA – ₹4.5 LPA depending on skills, certifications, industry, and location.

Experienced Salary

Experienced Electricians can earn ₹5 LPA – ₹12 LPA or more. Those working in industrial plants, power sectors, or overseas projects may earn significantly higher salaries.

Future Scope

The demand for Electricians continues to grow due to rapid urbanization, industrial development, renewable energy projects, and smart infrastructure. With experience and advanced training, Electricians can progress to supervisory, engineering, and contractor-level positions.
