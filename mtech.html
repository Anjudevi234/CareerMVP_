M.Tech

Master of Technology (M.Tech) is a postgraduate engineering degree focused on advanced technical knowledge, research, innovation and specialized engineering skills.

Brief Description

M.Tech is a two-year postgraduate degree designed for engineering graduates who wish to specialize in advanced technologies, research and development. It provides in-depth knowledge in various engineering disciplines and prepares students for high-level technical careers.

Fresher Salary

Fresh M.Tech graduates can earn ₹5 LPA – ₹12 LPA depending on specialization, research experience and employer.

Experienced Salary

Experienced professionals can earn ₹12 LPA – ₹50 LPA or more in Artificial Intelligence, Research & Development, Cloud Computing and Advanced Engineering.
